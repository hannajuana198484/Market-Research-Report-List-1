<p><p>The Automated Vertical Lift Module (AVLM) market comprises two main types: Single Level Delivery and Dual Level Delivery. Single Level Delivery systems optimize space by delivering items to a single access point, ideal for smaller operations or limited floor space. In contrast, Dual Level Delivery systems feature two separate delivery points, allowing for greater efficiency in larger facilities by enabling simultaneous access to multiple tiers. Both types enhance inventory management and streamline order fulfillment processes significantly in various industries.</p></p>
