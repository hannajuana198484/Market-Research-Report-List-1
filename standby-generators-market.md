<p><ul><li>Natural Gas or Liquid Propane</li><li>Air-Cooled vs. Liquid Cooled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Standby generators are classified into two main fuel types: natural gas and liquid propane. Natural gas is often preferred for its availability and lower operating costs, while liquid propane provides more energy flexibility. Additionally, standby generators are categorized into air-cooled and liquid-cooled systems. Air-cooled generators are suitable for smaller residential applications, offering easier installation and maintenance. In contrast, liquid-cooled generators are designed for larger demands, providing higher efficiency and reliability, making them ideal for commercial use or larger homes.</p></p>
