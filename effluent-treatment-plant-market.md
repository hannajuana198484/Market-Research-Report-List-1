<p><p>The Effluent Treatment Plant (ETP) market encompasses three primary treatment types: </p><p>1. **Preliminary Treatment** involves screening and sedimentation to remove large solids and grit, ensuring smooth operation downstream.</p><p>2. **Secondary or Biological Treatment** employs microbial processes to degrade organic matter, effectively reducing pollutant levels in wastewater.</p><p>3. **Tertiary or Advanced Treatment** focuses on further purification, often involving chemical processes and disinfection to eliminate remaining contaminants and pathogens, resulting in treated effluent suitable for reuse or safe discharge.</p></p>
