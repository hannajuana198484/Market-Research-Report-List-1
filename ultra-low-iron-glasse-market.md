<p><p>The ultra-low-iron glass market includes various types, primarily rolled glass and float glass. Rolled glass is produced by passing molten glass through rollers, creating a thinner, more uniform product with excellent optical clarity, ideal for applications like architecture and displays. Float glass, created by floating molten glass on molten tin, offers superior flatness and transparency, often used in windows and solar panels. Both types are essential for industries focusing on aesthetics and energy efficiency, driving their demand in modern construction and design.</p></p>
