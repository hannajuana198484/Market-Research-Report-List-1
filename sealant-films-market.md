<p><p>The sealant films market comprises two primary types: plastic sealant films and metal sealant films. Plastic sealant films, commonly made from materials like polyethylene and polypropylene, offer flexibility, moisture resistance, and cost-effectiveness, making them ideal for food packaging and various industrial applications. In contrast, metal sealant films, often aluminum-based, provide superior barrier properties against light, oxygen, and moisture, making them suitable for pharmaceutical products and high-value food items. Both types cater to diverse packaging needs across industries.</p></p>
