<p><ul><li> Powder</li><li>Solvent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>FEVE (Fluoroethylene Vinyl Ether) coating market is primarily divided into two types: powder and solvent-based coatings. Powder coatings are applied as a dry powder and cured under heat, offering a durable, eco-friendly finish with minimal VOC emissions. Solvent-based FEVE coatings, on the other hand, are liquid formulations that utilize solvents for application, providing excellent adhesion and flexibility. Both types enhance resistance to weathering, chemicals, and UV degradation, making them suitable for various industrial applications and outdoor environments.</p></p>
