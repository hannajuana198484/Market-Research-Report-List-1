<p><ul><li>Implantable Cardiac Monitors</li><li>Conventional Cardiac Monitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ICU cardiac monitors market is primarily divided into two types: implantable cardiac monitors and conventional cardiac monitors. </p><p>Implantable cardiac monitors are devices surgically embedded within a patient to continuously track heart rhythms and detect arrhythmias over extended periods. They offer real-time data collection and long-term monitoring. </p><p>Conversely, conventional cardiac monitors are typically used in hospital settings, providing immediate heart status updates through external leads and display screens. They are vital for acute care situations, delivering vital signs monitoring and alerts for clinicians.</p></p>
