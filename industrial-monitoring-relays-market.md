<p><p>Industrial monitoring relays encompass various types designed to ensure equipment safety and operational efficiency. Voltage monitoring relays protect systems from voltage fluctuations, preventing damage to sensitive components. Level monitoring relays gauge the levels of liquids or solids, preventing overflows and ensuring optimal inventory management. Current monitoring relays track electrical current to detect overloads or faults, safeguarding machinery and enhancing reliability. Together, these relays play a crucial role in maintaining system integrity across various industrial applications.</p></p>
