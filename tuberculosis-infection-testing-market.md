<p><p>The Tuberculosis Infection Testing Market consists of three primary types: Tuberculin Skin Test (TST), Interferon Gamma Release Assay (IGRA), and others. TST is a traditional method that involves an intradermal injection to detect immune responses to TB proteins. IGRA is a blood test that measures immune reactions to TB antigens, offering a more specific and faster alternative. Other methods may include nucleic acid amplification tests or diagnostic imaging, broadening testing capabilities depending on patient needs and clinical settings.</p></p>
