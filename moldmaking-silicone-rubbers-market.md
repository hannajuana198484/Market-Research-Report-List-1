<p><strong>The Moldmaking Silicone Rubbers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Silicone Rubber</li><li>Liquid Silicone Rubber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Moldmaking silicone rubbers are categorized primarily into solid silicone rubber and liquid silicone rubber (LSR). Solid silicone rubber is a dense, durable material suitable for creating rigid molds, offering excellent heat resistance and dimensional stability. In contrast, liquid silicone rubber is a versatile, two-part system that cures at room temperature, allowing for intricate detail reproduction and flexibility in mold design. Both types are widely used in various applications including industrial, automotive, and medical sectors due to their high performance and reliability.</p></p>
