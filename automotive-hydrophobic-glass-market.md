<p><p>The automotive hydrophobic glass market comprises two primary types: Hydrophobic Coating Technology and Nanofilm Technology. Hydrophobic Coating Technology involves applying a specialized chemical layer on the glass surface, which repels water and enhances visibility during rain. Nanofilm Technology, on the other hand, utilizes ultra-thin films that form a protective barrier, offering hydrophobic properties while also improving scratch resistance and durability. Both technologies significantly enhance driver safety and comfort by minimizing water accumulation and ensuring clearer vision in adverse weather conditions.</p></p>
