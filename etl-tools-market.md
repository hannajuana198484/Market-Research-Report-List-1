<p><p>ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) tools can be categorized into on-premise and cloud-based markets. On-premise ETL tools are installed locally within an organization’s infrastructure, offering greater control over data security, customization, and integration with legacy systems. However, they require significant maintenance and upfront investment. Conversely, cloud-based ETL tools operate on a subscription model and provide scalability, flexibility, and quicker deployment. They enable organizations to access and process data from anywhere, facilitating remote collaboration and reducing infrastructure costs.</p></p>
