<p><p>Ambient food packaging refers to packaging solutions designed to preserve food products at room temperature, without the need for refrigeration or freezing. It is commonly used for a wide range of food items, including snacks, ready-to-eat meals, and processed foods.</p><p>The Ambient Food Packaging Market is experiencing significant growth, primarily driven by increasing consumer demand for convenience foods, on-the-go meal options, and longer shelf life.
