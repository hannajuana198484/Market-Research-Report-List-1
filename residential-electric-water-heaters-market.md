<p><p>The residential electric water heaters market primarily includes two types: tankless electric water heaters and tank electric water heaters. Tankless water heaters provide on-demand hot water, heating it as needed without storing it, making them energy-efficient and space-saving. In contrast, tank electric water heaters store a pre-heated supply of water in a tank, offering a steady supply but consuming more energy to maintain temperature. Both types cater to different user preferences and requirements regarding space, efficiency, and hot water availability.</p></p>
