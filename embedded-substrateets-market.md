<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Embedded Substrate(ETS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Embedded Passive Substrate(EPS)</li><li>Embedded Trace Substrate(ETS)</li><li>Embedded Dies Substrate(EDS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Embedded Substrate (ETS) market encompasses several types of substrates designed for advanced electronic packaging. Embedded Passive Substrate (EPS) integrates passive components like capacitors and resistors within the substrate to reduce space and enhance performance. Embedded Trace Substrate (ETS) features embedded interconnects that facilitate miniaturization and improved signal integrity. Embedded Dies Substrate (EDS) incorporates active chips directly into the substrate, enhancing functionality and thermal management. Together, these substrates enable compact, efficient designs in high-performance electronics.</p></p>
