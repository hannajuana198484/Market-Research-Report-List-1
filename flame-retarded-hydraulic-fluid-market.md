<p><p>The flame-retarded hydraulic fluid market comprises four main types: HFA, HFB, HFC, and HFD. HFA fluids are water-containing and offer low flammability. HFB fluids, also water-containing, provide moderate fire resistance. HFC fluids are water-based but contain synthetic substances to enhance fire safety, making them less flammable than HFA and HFB. HFD fluids are non-water-containing and provide high fire resistance, suitable for extreme conditions. Each type caters to specific operational needs in various industrial applications.</p></p>
