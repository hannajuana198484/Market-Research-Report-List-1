<p><p>The Hollow Fiber Tangential Flow Cartridge market is segmented into two main types: microfiltration and ultrafiltration. Microfiltration utilizes hollow fiber membranes with larger pore sizes to remove suspended solids, bacteria, and large colloids, making it suitable for water treatment and clarification processes. In contrast, ultrafiltration employs smaller pore sizes, allowing it to effectively separate macromolecules, viruses, and smaller particles, making it ideal for applications such as beverage and pharmaceutical filtration. Both technologies enhance separation efficiency in various industries.</p></p>
