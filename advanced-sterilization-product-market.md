<p><p>The Advanced Sterilization Product market encompasses various sterilization methods, each suited for specific applications. Dry heat sterilization utilizes high temperatures to eliminate microorganisms, ideal for heat-resistant items. Ethylene oxide is a gas-based method effective for sensitive equipment, ensuring thorough penetration without damage. E-Beam sterilization employs electron beams for rapid disinfection, suitable for medical device sterilization. Steam sterilization, or autoclaving, uses saturated steam to sterilize, making it the most common and effective method for surgical instruments and laboratory tools.</p></p>
