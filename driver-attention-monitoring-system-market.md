<p><p>A Driver Attention Monitoring System (DAMS) is an advanced automotive safety technology designed to detect driver fatigue, distraction, or drowsiness. It uses sensors, cameras, and AI algorithms to monitor driver behavior, such as eye movements, head position, and steering patterns. If signs of inattention are detected, the system alerts the driver through visual, auditory, or haptic feedback, enhancing road safety and reducing the risk of accidents.</p><p>The Driver Attention Monitoring System Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.7% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by increasing demand for advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS), stricter safety regulations, and rising awareness about road safety. The integration of AI and machine learning in DAMS is further enhancing its accuracy and reliability, making it a key component in modern vehicles.</p><p>Emerging trends in the market include the adoption of infrared cameras for better performance in low-light conditions, the development of multi-modal systems combining facial recognition and biometric sensors, and the integration of DAMS with autonomous driving technologies. Additionally, the growing popularity of electric and connected vehicles is expected to boost market expansion. North America, Europe, and Asia-Pacific are leading regions in terms of adoption, with Asia-Pacific showing significant growth potential due to increasing vehicle production and urbanization.</p></p>
