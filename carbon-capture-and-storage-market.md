<p><strong>The Carbon Capture and Storage Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre-Combustion Carbon Capture</li><li>Oxy-Combustion Carbon Capture</li><li>Post-Combustion Carbon Capture</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The **Carbon Capture and Storage (CCS)** market includes three primary types:  </p><p>1. **Pre-Combustion Carbon Capture**: Extracts CO₂ before fuel combustion, converting fossil fuels into hydrogen and CO₂, which is then captured.  </p><p>2. **Oxy-Combustion Carbon Capture**: Burns fuel in pure oxygen, producing a concentrated CO₂ stream for easier capture.  </p><p>3. **Post-Combustion Carbon Capture**: Captures CO₂ from flue gases after combustion using chemical solvents or adsorbents.  </p><p>Each method targets reducing emissions, with applications in power generation, industrial processes, and energy production.</p></p>
