<p><p>Inertial systems for the aerospace market encompass various technologies used for navigation and motion sensing. AHRS (Attitude and Heading Reference Systems) provide orientation data, while INS (Inertial Navigation Systems) offer precise positioning. IMUs (Inertial Measurement Units) measure acceleration and angular velocity, forming the backbone of inertial measurements. Laser-based systems utilize laser technology for high-accuracy positioning and distance measurements. Other systems may include hybrid or specialized solutions tailored for specific aerospace applications, enhancing navigation and stability in aircraft and spacecraft.</p></p>
