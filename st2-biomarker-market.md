<p><p>The ST2 biomarker market is primarily categorized into ELISA-based and immunochromatography-based testing methods. ELISA-based ST2 biomarkers utilize enzyme-linked immunosorbent assays to quantitatively measure ST2 protein levels, offering high sensitivity and specificity for clinical applications. In contrast, immunochromatography-based ST2 biomarkers employ a rapid, strip-based format for qualitative detection, making it suitable for point-of-care settings. Both types serve critical roles in cardiovascular disease management, driving advancements in diagnostics and patient monitoring.</p></p>
