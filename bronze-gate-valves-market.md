<p><ul><li>Flanged Connection</li><li>Threaded Connection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bronze Gate Valves market is segmented into two primary connection types: flanged and threaded connections. Flanged gate valves feature a flat surface with bolt holes, allowing easy installation and removal, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. In contrast, threaded gate valves come with male or female threads, providing a secure, leak-proof seal, ideal for smaller pipelines and space-constrained environments. Both types serve various industrial sectors, ensuring fluid control and regulation in piping systems.</p></p>
