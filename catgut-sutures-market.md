<p><p>The catgut sutures market is categorized into two primary types: Chromic Catgut Sutures and Plain Catgut Sutures. Chromic catgut sutures are treated with chromium salts to enhance their tensile strength and prolong absorption time, making them suitable for procedures requiring extended wound support. In contrast, plain catgut sutures are absorbed more quickly and are ideal for short-term applications. Both types are derived from natural sources and are widely used in surgical procedures for their biocompatibility and effectiveness.</p></p>
