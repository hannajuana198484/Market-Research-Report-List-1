<p><p>High-temperature superconductor (HTS) cables are categorized into various types based on their composition. YBCO cables, made from Yttrium Barium Copper Oxide, are known for high performance and critical current capacity. Bi-2212 cables, composed of Bismuth Strontium Calcium Copper Oxide, offer flexibility and are suitable for medium-scale applications. Bi-2223 cables, a more advanced variant, provide enhanced conductivity and thermal limits, making them ideal for large-scale power systems. Each type serves specific needs in efficient power transmission and advanced technologies.</p></p>
