<p><p>The Wireless WiFi 6 Router market features three main types: Single Band, Dual Band, and Tri Band routers. Single Band routers operate on a single frequency, typically 2.4 GHz, suitable for basic internet usage. Dual Band routers utilize both 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz frequencies, offering better speed and reduced interference, ideal for moderate to heavy usage. Tri Band routers add an extra 5 GHz band to handle multiple devices efficiently, making them suitable for high-demand environments like gaming and streaming.</p></p>
