<p><strong>The Low Iron Solar Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rolled Glass</li><li>Float Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low iron solar glass market primarily includes two types: rolled glass and float glass. Rolled glass is manufactured through a process that involves heating and rolling, resulting in superior optical clarity and reduced iron content, enhancing light transmission for solar applications. Float glass, on the other hand, is produced by floating molten glass on molten tin, yielding a smooth, uniform surface ideal for solar panels. Both types play a crucial role in improving solar panel efficiency and overall energy output.</p></p>
