<p><p>Biogas upgrading equipment comprises various technologies that enhance the quality of biogas by removing impurities and enriching methane content. Water scrubbers use water as a solvent to absorb carbon dioxide and other contaminants. Chemical scrubbers employ chemical reactions to achieve purification, targeting specific gases efficiently. Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA) utilizes pressure variations to selectively adsorb impurities, while membrane technology separates gases based on their molecular size and permeability. Each method offers distinct advantages, catering to different operational needs and scales.</p></p>
