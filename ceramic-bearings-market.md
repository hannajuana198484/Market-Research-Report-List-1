<p><p>Ceramic bearings are categorized mainly into three types: ZrO2 (Zirconium Dioxide), Si3N4 (Silicon Nitride), and other materials. ZrO2 bearings are known for their high toughness, corrosion resistance, and thermal stability, making them ideal for harsh environments. Si3N4 bearings offer superior wear resistance and low density, suitable for high-speed applications. Other ceramic materials may include alumina and composite ceramics, each providing distinct properties for specific industrial uses, enhancing performance, reliability, and extending service life in various applications.</p></p>
