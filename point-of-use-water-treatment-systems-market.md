<p><p>Point-of-Use Water Treatment Systems encompass several market types, each addressing specific water quality issues. Reverse Osmosis utilizes a semipermeable membrane to remove contaminants, ensuring clean drinking water. Distillation involves boiling water to separate impurities, effectively eliminating pathogens and minerals. Disinfection employs chemical or physical methods, such as UV light, to kill harmful microorganisms and enhance safety. Filtration systems use various media to physically remove particles, sediments, and some contaminants, improving water clarity and taste, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
