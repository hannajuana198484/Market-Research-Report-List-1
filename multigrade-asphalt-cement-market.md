<p><p>The multigrade asphalt cement market includes two primary types: polymer-modified asphalt and synthetic resin-modified asphalt. Polymer-modified asphalt utilizes polymers to enhance elasticity, durability, and temperature stability, making it suitable for various climates and traffic conditions. Synthetic resin-modified asphalt incorporates synthetic resins to improve adhesion and resistance to aging and deformation. Both types play a crucial role in road construction and maintenance, providing improved performance characteristics and extending the lifespan of asphalt pavements.</p></p>
