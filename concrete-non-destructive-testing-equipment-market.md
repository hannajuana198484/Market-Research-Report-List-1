<p><p>The Concrete Non-Destructive Testing Equipment market includes various types of devices designed to assess the integrity and quality of concrete without causing damage. Concrete test hammers measure hardness and estimate compressive strength. Ultrasonic pulse velocity testers evaluate the material's uniformity and detect flaws by sending sound waves through concrete. Pull-off adhesion testers assess the bond strength of coatings or overlays. Other equipment may include embedment gauges, rebound hammers, or moisture meters, broadening the evaluation capabilities for concrete structures.</p></p>
