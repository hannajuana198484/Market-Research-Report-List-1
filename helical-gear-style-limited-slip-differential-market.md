<p><p>The Helical-gear Style Limited-slip Differential (LSD) market features primarily two types: Mechanical LSD and Electronic LSD. Mechanical LSD utilizes a system of helical gears and clutch plates to distribute power between wheels, enhancing traction during cornering and off-road driving. Conversely, Electronic LSD employs sensors and braking interventions to dynamically manage torque distribution based on real-time conditions, offering improved control and adaptability. Both types aim to optimize vehicle performance and stability, catering to a range of automotive applications.</p></p>
