<p><p>The digital insulin cooler market is categorized into two main types: powered insulin coolers and non-powered insulin coolers. Powered insulin coolers utilize refrigeration technology and energy sources like battery or AC power to maintain a stable temperature for insulin storage. In contrast, non-powered insulin coolers rely on insulation or phase change materials to provide temperature control without active cooling. Both types aim to ensure that insulin remains effective by preventing temperature fluctuations, catering to different user preferences and needs.</p></p>
