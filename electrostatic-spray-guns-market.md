<p><p>Electrostatic spray guns are advanced painting tools that use electrostatic charges to enhance the efficiency and quality of coating applications. By applying a positive charge to the paint particles, these guns attract them to the negatively charged substrates, ensuring a uniform and complete coating with minimal overspray. This technology is widely used in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, furniture, and electronics, due to its ability to achieve high transfer efficiency and reduce material waste.</p><p>The Electrostatic Spray Guns Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.2% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for efficient coating solutions and stringent environmental regulations favoring low-solvent coatings. Key trends influencing market growth include technological advancements such as the development of automated and smart spray guns, rising adoption in industrial applications, and a growing emphasis on sustainability. Furthermore, the expansion of the automotive sector in emerging economies and innovations in coatings are also contributing to the market's expansion.
