<p><p>Molecular sieve activation powders are classified primarily into two types: A Type and X Type. A Type molecular sieves, characterized by their 3A and 4A variants, are primarily used for drying applications and separating smaller molecules like water and ammonia. In contrast, X Type molecular sieves, which feature larger pore sizes, are effective for removing larger molecules and can serve in gas separation and catalysis. These distinctions guide their application in industries such as petrochemical, food processing, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
