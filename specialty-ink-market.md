<p><p>The specialty ink market encompasses innovative inks like thermochromic and photochromic types, each offering unique functionalities. Thermochromic inks change color in response to temperature variations, making them popular in security labels and mood rings. In contrast, photochromic inks react to UV light, transitioning colors when exposed to sunlight, often used in eyewear and packaging. Both inks provide interactive features, enhancing product appeal and functionality, and are increasingly utilized in various industries, including consumer goods, packaging, and digital applications.</p></p>
