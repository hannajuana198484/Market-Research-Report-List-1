<p><ul><li>X-ray Scanner</li><li>Millimeter Wave Scanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airport baggage scanner market comprises primarily two types: X-ray scanners and millimeter wave scanners. X-ray scanners utilize ionizing radiation to penetrate luggage, producing images that reveal potentially harmful items. They are widely used for their effectiveness and established technology. Conversely, millimeter wave scanners employ non-ionizing radio waves, creating a 3D image of the contents without exposing users to radiation. This technology is gaining popularity for its safety profile and ability to detect concealed threats, enhancing airport security overall.</p></p>
