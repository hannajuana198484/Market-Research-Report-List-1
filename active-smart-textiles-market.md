<p><strong>The Active Smart Textiles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resistive Sensor</li><li>Capacitive Sensor</li><li>Piezoresistive Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The active smart textiles market includes various sensor types: resistive sensors, capacitive sensors, and piezoresistive sensors. Resistive sensors change resistance based on strain or pressure, commonly used in wearable devices. Capacitive sensors measure changes in capacitance due to pressure or proximity, enabling touch-sensitive applications. Piezoresistive sensors alter electrical resistance in response to mechanical stress, making them ideal for monitoring physical movements. These sensor technologies enhance functionality in textiles for health monitoring, sports performance, and interactive clothing applications.</p></p>
