<p><ul><li>Gearless Traction Machine</li><li>Geared Traction Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The escalator traction machine market is primarily divided into two types: gearless and geared traction machines. Gearless traction machines use direct drive systems, providing higher efficiency and smoother operation with reduced maintenance due to fewer moving parts. In contrast, geared traction machines utilize a gearbox to amplify torque, catering to specific applications where lower initial costs are prioritized. Both types serve distinct segments, with gearless machines favored for modern escalators requiring high performance, while geared machines are often employed in conventional settings.</p></p>
