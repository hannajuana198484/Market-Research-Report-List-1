<p><p>The Vehicle Hydraulic Brake Fluid Market includes types like DOT3, DOT4, DOT5, and others, each with distinct properties. DOT3 is glycol-based, affordable, and suitable for standard vehicles but has a lower boiling point. DOT4 offers higher performance with a higher boiling point, ideal for heavy-duty or high-performance vehicles. DOT5 is silicone-based, non-hygroscopic, and used in specialized applications but incompatible with ABS systems. Other types cater to niche requirements, balancing performance, compatibility, and environmental factors for diverse automotive needs.</p></p>
