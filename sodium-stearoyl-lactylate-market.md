<p><p>Sodium Stearoyl Lactylate (SSL) is a versatile food additive widely used as an emulsifier, stabilizer, and dough conditioner in the food industry. It is derived from the esterification of stearic acid and lactic acid, followed by neutralization with sodium hydroxide. SSL enhances the texture, shelf life, and consistency of baked goods, dairy products, and processed foods. Its ability to improve dough strength, volume, and crumb structure makes it a key ingredient in bread and bakery products.</p><p>The Sodium Stearoyl Lactylate Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for convenience foods, bakery products, and processed foods globally.
