<p><strong>The Glass Coatings for Containers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot-end Coatings</li><li>Cold-end Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Glass coatings for containers are categorized into hot-end and cold-end coatings, each serving distinct purposes. Hot-end coatings are applied during the manufacturing process while the glass is still molten, enhancing thermal resistance, and providing protection against corrosion and thermal shock. Cold-end coatings, on the other hand, are applied after the glass has cooled. They focus on improving surface properties, such as scratch resistance and barrier performance, enhancing aesthetics, and prolonging shelf life for products like beverages and food containers.</p></p>
