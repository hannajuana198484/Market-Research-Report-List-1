<p><p>The Digital Demodulator IC market can be segmented into single-channel and dual-channel types. Single-channel demodulators are designed to process one signal at a time, making them ideal for applications requiring simplicity and lower power consumption. In contrast, dual-channel demodulators can handle two signals simultaneously, enhancing data throughput and efficiency in more complex systems. This versatility in channel configuration allows manufacturers to choose ICs based on their specific use cases, such as telecommunications, broadcasting, and data communication applications.</p></p>
