<p><p>The Automatic Vehicle Barrier Gate market primarily includes two types: straight and crank barrier gates. Straight barrier gates feature a horizontal arm that opens and closes directly upward, suitable for rapid vehicle access. Crank barrier gates utilize a mechanical arm that pivots to a side, often preferred in areas with limited vertical space or specific design requirements. Both types enhance security and traffic control at entry points, catering to diverse settings such as parking lots, toll booths, and restricted access areas.</p></p>
