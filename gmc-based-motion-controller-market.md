<p><p>GMC-based motion controllers are classified into three market types: PLC-based, PC-based, and stand-alone. PLC-based controllers integrate motion control within programmable logic controllers, ideal for factory automation. PC-based controllers utilize powerful computing resources to manage complex motion tasks and offer extensive customization. Stand-alone controllers operate independently, designed for specific applications without the need for external systems, ensuring ease of use and quick deployment. Each type caters to different industrial needs, balancing functionality, complexity, and integration capabilities.</p></p>
