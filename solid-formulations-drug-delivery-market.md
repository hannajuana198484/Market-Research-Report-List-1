<p><p>The solid formulations drug delivery market primarily includes powders and suppositories. Powders are medication in dry form that can be mixed with liquids or taken directly, offering flexibility in dosing and administration. Suppositories, on the other hand, are solid dosage forms designed to be inserted into body cavities, where they dissolve and release active ingredients for systemic or localized effects. Both forms provide unique advantages in terms of stability, dosage accuracy, and patient compliance compared to liquid formulations.</p></p>
