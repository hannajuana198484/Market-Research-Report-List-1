<p><strong>The Medical Hygiene Non Woven Fabric Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drylaid</li><li>Airlaid</li><li>Wetlaid</li><li>Spunlaid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical hygiene non-woven fabric market is categorized into four main types: Drylaid, Airlaid, Wetlaid, and Spunlaid. Drylaid involves laying fibers dry and bonding them through methods like thermal or chemical processes. Airlaid uses air to distribute fibers, creating a soft, absorbent fabric ideal for hygiene products. Wetlaid combines fibers with water, enhancing strength and uniformity, suitable for medical applications. Spunlaid utilizes high-velocity air to produce fine, strong fabrics commonly used in surgical masks and gowns.</p></p>
